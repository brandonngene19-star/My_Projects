Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my account after payment?

All accounts are delivered instantly! You'll receive your login credentials immediately after your payment is confirmed. We use automated delivery systems to ensure you get access within minutes.

Are these shared accounts or personal accounts?

We provide premium shared accounts that are fully functional. Each account is limited to a specific number of users to ensure optimal performance and reliability for all our customers.

What happens if my account stops working?

We offer a 30-day warranty on all accounts. If your account experiences any issues within this period, we'll provide a replacement immediately. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, cryptocurrency, and popular local payment options. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I upgrade or change my service later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between services at any time. Contact our support team, and we'll help you make the changes seamlessly.
